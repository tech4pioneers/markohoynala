About Me

I drive projects to
the finish line.

I'm a result-driven executive, winner of 3 innovation competitions, author of 6 patents and a serial entrepreneur with over 25 years of industry experience.

I help organizations turn complex ideas into real-world results. My work spans inspiring keynote talks as well as hands-on advisory and execution support, tailored to where your business is today and where it needs to go next.

I spent 20 years in the telecommunications industry (1995–2015) in a variety of senior leadership roles at Nokia, Renesas and Broadcom, working at scale within large, complex and multi-site organizations across four countries.

Having lived and worked in Finland, India, UK and Sweden, I adapt quickly to new business contexts, cultures, and teams. I'm known as a leader who drives initiatives and projects always to completion.
